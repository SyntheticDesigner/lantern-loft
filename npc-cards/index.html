Torik Norri is the son of Vorik and Selva Norri. Following in his father's footsteps he joined the guard as soon as he was of age. He defends the western roads and woodlands. Though his job has become more difficult as of late, and the roads more dangerous, he remains an optimist.

Torik is an optimist, and generally tries to maintain a sunny disposition..

Torik often tries to assist other areas of the guard. Not only his troop along the western road.

Torik has a habit of over-simplifying situations.

Torik will risk his life to save others without pause if he believes he can save them.
